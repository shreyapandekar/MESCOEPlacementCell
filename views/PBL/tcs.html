Eligibility Criteria:
1. Certification in several Programming Languages will be considered a bonus.
2. The candidate must secure minimum aggregate marks of 60% or above.
3. An overall gap of two years in the academic year is permitted.
4. It is mandatory for candidates to mention the gaps/backlogs during their academic and/or work experience.
5. The age limit is defined as a minimum of 18 years and a maximum of 28 years.
